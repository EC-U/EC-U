            Prospectus
           
          
         
        
       
       
        
         
         
         
         
         
         
         
          
           
            
             
              
               
                
                 1、
                
               
              
              
               团体赛采用3人对抗形式，男、女不限。每场比赛每个运动员只能上场一次。第一阶段的团体赛采用分组循环赛，需打满三盘比赛。每小组取一名或两名进入第二阶段（视报名情况而定）。第二阶段比赛采用单淘汰制，实行三盘二胜制，分别决出前八名（并列第三名，并列第五名）。团体赛全部比赛采用三局二胜11分制。
              
              
               
                Mixed team matches have preliminary round of contests. Each school and organization may have up to two teams, and each team has four players. Mixed team match is in the form of 3 vs 3 (1 player as substitute), regardless of man and woman. Each player can only participant in one game. First round uses robin round matches and all teams are required to finish all three matches. Each team will have one or two players moved to second round (the number of players moved to next round is decided by the final number of applicants). Second round uses elimination system as best two out of three games. Top 8 will be ranked after second round with ties in Third and Fifth Place. All mixed team matches will adopt the best two out of three games and 11 points each game.
               
              
              
               
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
               
                
                 2、
                
               
               
               
              
              
               男、女单打比赛均采用单淘汰的比赛方式进行，由电脑抽签决定比赛秩序。比赛采用一局15分制。分别决出男、女组前八名，采用并列第三名；并列第五名。报名不足17人决出前六名，不足9人决出前四名。
              
              
               
                Both men’s singles and women’s singles are in elimination system. Computer decides the order of the games. Singles competition will adopt the best two out of three games and 15 points each game. The competition will rank Top 8 for both men and women sections respectively with ties in Third and Fifth Place. (Top 6 will be ranked when the applicants are fewer than 17 people and Top 4 will be ranked when the applicants are fewer than 9 people).

                 1) 演讲环节：每人4-5分钟
                
               
              
              
               
                
                 Speech Session: 4-5 minutes per person
                
               
               
                
                
                
                
               
               
              
              
               每位参赛选手以“我的中国故事”为主题进行演讲。演讲内容可结合自身在华师大学习生活的经历和感受，以一个小故事或者一个真实发生的事件为原型展开一定的思考和感悟；演讲内容应积极向上，以中文进行，脱稿完成。
              
              
               
                Each participant will give a speech on the theme of "My China Story". The content of the speech can be combined with the students' experience and feelings in ECNU, Take a small story or a real event as the prototype to start a speech. The content of the speech should be positive, carried out in Chinese, and finished off the script.
               
              
              
               
              
              
               
                
                 2）互动环节：每人2-3分钟
                
               
              
              
               
                
                 Interaction: 2-3 minutes per person

                After each participant's speech, the judges will ask questions. The questions may be related to the content of the contestants' speeches, or may be related to the knowledge of Chinese society and culture.

            赛制规则
           
           
            Prospectus
           
          
         
        
       
       
        
         
         
         
         
         
         
         
          
           
            
             
              
               
                
                 1、
                
               
              
              
               团体赛采用3人对抗形式，男、女不限。每场比赛每个运动员只能上场一次。第一阶段的团体赛采用分组循环赛，需打满三盘比赛。每小组取一名或两名进入第二阶段（视报名情况而定）。第二阶段比赛采用单淘汰制，实行三盘二胜制，分别决出前八名（并列第三名，并列第五名）。团体赛全部比赛采用三局二胜11分制。
              
              
               
                Mixed team matches have preliminary round of contests. Each school and organization may have up to two teams, and each team has four players. Mixed team match is in the form of 3 vs 3 (1 player as substitute), regardless of man and woman. Each player can only participant in one game. First round uses robin round matches and all teams are required to finish all three matches. Each team will have one or two players moved to second round (the number of players moved to next round is decided by the final number of applicants). Second round uses elimination system as best two out of three games. Top 8 will be ranked after second round with ties in Third and Fifth Place. All mixed team matches will adopt the best two out of three games and 11 points each game.
               
              
              
               
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
               
                
                 2、
                
               
               
               
              
              
               男、女单打比赛均采用单淘汰的比赛方式进行，由电脑抽签决定比赛秩序。比赛采用一局15分制。分别决出男、女组前八名，采用并列第三名；并列第五名。报名不足17人决出前六名，不足9人决出前四名。
              
              
               
                Both men’s singles and women’s singles are in elimination system. Computer decides the order of the games. Singles competition will adopt the best two out of three games and 15 points each game. The competition will rank Top 8 for both men and women sections respectively with ties in Third and Fifth Place. (Top 6 will be ranked when the applicants are fewer than 17 people and Top 4 will be ranked when the applicants are fewer than 9 people).
               
              
              
               
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
             
            
           
           
            
             
              
               
                
                 3、
                
               
               
               
              
              
               为了增加快乐乒乓、趣味乒乓的氛围，主办方将在中午和赛事间歇，现场邀请嘉宾和现场接受参赛选手的报名，进行有奖趣味乒乓活动。
              
              
               
               
              
              
               
                Guests and audiences will be invited to participate in interest games with prize during lunch break and in-between games so as to increase the fun.
